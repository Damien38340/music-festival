This Is Not A Love Song

This Is Not A Love Song festival takes place from Thursday May 30 to Saturday June 1 at Paloma. This year, This Is Not A Love Song celebrates its 7th anniversary. 3 days, 5 stages and a program as demanding as ever, with 55 international artists and independent music groups to discover at the end of May. TINALS is also all about participatory workshops, a unique and daring festival atmosphere and experience.
